Emily Wong

About

Emily Wong is a senior at Carlmont High School. She participated in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Electronic Arts in the summer of 2015, and created and is president of the Girls Who Code club at her school. It was from the Girls Who Code program that Emily grew her love of coding, and has continued working on it ever since.

In addition to computer science, Emily participates in musical theater, and has been in 4 shows at her high school, as well as 11 shows with San Carlos Children's Theater. She also volunteers as a tutor for an hour each week to a middle school girl, and volunteers as a "buddy" for the AYSO VIP program, which helps children with disabilities learn how to play soccer.

The purpose of this website is to demonstrate her computer science abilites and give easy and simple ways to get in touch for business inquiries, etc.
